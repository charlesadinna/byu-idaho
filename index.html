Lagos, Nigeria

Lagos, nestled along Nigeria's southwestern coastline, is not only Nigeria's largest city but also the most populous in Africa, boasting a population of over 20 million people. It's a melting pot of cultures, languages, and traditions, with residents hailing from various ethnic groups across Nigeria and beyond, contributing to its vibrant cultural tapestry. Moreover, Lagos is home to Nollywood, the Nigerian film industry, which produces more movies annually than Hollywood, making it a hub for entertainment and creativity. Despite its challenges, Lagos remains a beacon of opportunity, drawing migrants from all over Nigeria and beyond seeking a better life in its bustling streets and vibrant markets.
